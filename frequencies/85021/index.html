Radio Frequencies for Maricopa County, Arizona (85021)

ZIP 85021 lies in central Phoenix, AZ (Maricopa County). It is fully covered by the statewide P25 Phase II system (AZWIN) and the City of Phoenix Regional Wireless Cooperative (RWC) system. Conventional analog channels remain in use for fireground, mutual-aid, and specialty services. All primary public-safety dispatching is trunked.
