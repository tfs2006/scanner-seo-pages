Radio Frequencies for Bexar County, Texas (78213)

Radio frequencies for the 78213 ZIP code, located in Bexar County, Texas, which includes the city of San Antonio and Castle Hills. Public safety operations for San Antonio and Bexar County primarily utilize the Alamo Area Regional Radio System (AARRS), a Project 25 Phase II trunked system. San Antonio Police are fully encrypted, while the Bexar County Sheriff operates mostly encrypted but sometimes in the clear. Bexar County Fire Alarm dispatches many volunteer fire departments (VFDs) on VHF frequencies.
