Radio Frequencies for Monmouth County, New Jersey (07738)

Lincroft (07738) is part of Middletown Township. Public safety agencies (Police, Fire, EMS) primarily operate on the Monmouth County Public Safety P25 Phase II trunked system. Law enforcement talkgroups are largely encrypted (Mode TE). Conventional VHF frequencies are maintained for Fire/EMS paging and as backup channels.
