Radio Frequencies for Roanoke County, Virginia (24001)

Roanoke County, Virginia uses a mix of analog and digital trunked radio systems, including the Roanoke Valley Radio System (RVRS) and the Virginia Statewide Agencies Radio System (STARS). Public safety services like police and fire are often encrypted. Conventional frequencies are also in use for various services.
