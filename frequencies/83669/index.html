Radio Frequencies for Ada County, Idaho (83669)

Ada County, Idaho, including the city of Star, utilizes a mix of conventional and trunked radio systems for public safety and other services. The Idaho Cooperative Agencies Wireless Interoperable Network (ICAWIN) is a significant P25 trunked system in use. Ada County SO and Boise PD have migrated to ICAWIN. Fire frequencies may be simulcast on VHF and 700 MHz.
